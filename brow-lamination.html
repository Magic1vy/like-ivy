Brow Lamination

Eyebrow lamination is the same as long-term styling, which allows you to fix the hairs in the desired position, give a beautiful shape to unruly eyebrows and eliminate the need to use fixing gels every day. For the next up to two month, the eyebrows will look freshly combed - and very natural.

You can add a strengthening BOTOX to make your brows stronger and enhance brow growth.

Timing: 1-1,5 hour

Price: $130

Price with BOTOX: $150

Step 1

The procedure usually consists of four stages: first, a softening compound is applied to the eyebrows, which makes the hair pliable.

Step 2

The second stage of the procedure is the actual fixation. The composition will be applied to the eyebrows, then they will give them the desired shape - and leave it there for a while. When the composition is washed off, the hairs will remain in the same position.

Step 3

The third stage is coloring and shaping. As a result, the eyebrows look very natural and take on a completely new look.
