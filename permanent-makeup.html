Permanent Makeup

Permanent makeup (micropigmentation) is the introduction of a special pigment into the upper layers of the dermis using a needle. To accentuate, highlight, enhance and correct certain facial features and/or imitate regular makeup.

The pigment fits into the upper layers of the dermis, which is why, after a few years, high-quality permanent makeup comes off (this is influenced by the processes of skin renewal, the faster it is renewed, the faster the color fades).

⠀

According to a number of sources, permanent makeup began to be worn in the early 1900s in the UK, with tattoo artists Sutherland MacDonald and George Barchett behind it. It has become popular in the United States since the 1920s.

Powder Brows

Powdered brows are a state-of-the-art micropigmentation technique that produces a natural looking brow.

During the procedure, the artist introduces pigment into the upper layers of the skin. An imitation of makeup is created, as if shadows or a pencil were shaded gently and gently on the skin of the eyebrows. This technique is suitable for absolutely everyone and has no age restrictions.

    It is recommended to make powdery eyebrows if:

  • Your eyebrows are too thick, but there are bald spots in some areas;
  • Your eyebrows are too light and you need to give them color;
  • Your eyebrows fade quickly in the sun;
  • Your eyebrows grow chaotically and you want to give them a more neat and beautiful shape;
  • You don't want to waste time on makeup every day.

The main advantage of micropigmentation is that with its help you can forget for a long time about the need to tint the eyebrows with decorative cosmetics, during the day you do not worry if it has worn off or if it has flowed. Thanks to the powdery eyebrows, the make-up is perfect anytime, anywhere, under any circumstances.

Lash Line

Lash Line is a type of micropigmentation that is almost invisible to outsiders, while the look is given great expressiveness. The pigment is applied directly along the lash line between the hairs, it is not washed off with water and makeup removers. This feature allows you to completely abandon the use of decorative cosmetics or spend much less time on makeup.

Permanent makeup lash line solves the problem of girls whose skin is prone to allergic rashes. For many women, the lash line is the only way to improve their appearance, since the use of mascara, pencils and eye shadows cause tearing, swelling and other signs of allergies.

This type of permanent makeup will suit girls of any color type. He will emphasize the depth of the look. The eyelash line is the best solution for girls with sparse and light eyelashes, for those leading an active lifestyle. The permanent will help hide the first age-related changes and open your eyes.

Aquarelle Lips

Aquarelle lips are a type of permanent makeup that involves the use of a watercolor technique. Thanks to layer-by-layer application, shading and selection of natural pigments, it turns out to create a natural shade.

Usually, an artist uses several types of pigments, creating the most beautiful mix, taking into account the wishes of the client. Before the procedure, a local numb is applied to the lips, so the procedure is comfortable.
